Miloš Crnjanski

Miloš Crnjanski (October 1893 – 30 November 1977) was a poet of the expressionist wing of Serbian modernism, author, and a diplomat. From his beginnings as a journalist whose social-political stance was at one moment openly opposed to freedom and progress, he gradually arose to become a poet and romanticist.

Crnjanski was born in Csongrad, Hungary, to an impoverished family which moved in 1896 to Temesvar (Romania), where he grew up in a patriarchal-patriotic community with the implanted cult of Serbia and Serbian heritage in his soul as a precious relic.
